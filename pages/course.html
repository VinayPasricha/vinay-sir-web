Executive Education

AI For Business Leaders

How to Build Intelligent, Self-Learning Organizations. A 5-week founder-led course designed for senior leaders who want to move beyond AI hype and build real intelligence into their companies.

Overview

Course at a Glance

  • Format: 5 Weeks • 1 Class/Week • 90 Minutes Each
  • Audience: Harvard MBA / Executive Education
  • Delivery: Founder-led • Interactive • Case-Based • Hands-on Tools
  • Capstone: AI Advantage Map — 5 actionable AI opportunities for your business
Week 1

The AI Advantage — How Intelligent Organizations Think

Core Aim: Move from "AI tools" to "AI as a system of intelligence inside a company."

The 5 Dimensions of Intelligence

  • Cognition — How the organization perceives information
  • Execution — Speed & precision of actions
  • Data — What the business knows & how well organized
  • Governance — Ethics, controls, rules
  • Experience — Human trust & usability

The AI Brain

  • Knowledge Ingestion: Docs, SOPs, policies, CRM, conversations → unified knowledge
  • Inference Engine: How AI reasons and solves business questions
  • Orchestration Layer: Connecting tools, APIs, workflows
  • Self-Learning Feedback Loops: The system gets smarter with every interaction

Deliverables: Business Map across the 5 Dimensions • 2–3 AI improvement opportunities • Start of AI Advantage Map

Week 2

Toolpower — The AI Stack & Hands-On with Real Tools

Core Aim: Show students exactly how to build the AI Brain using modern tools.

The Modern AI Stack

  • Capture Layer: Fathom, Revio → capture calls, feedback, conversations
  • Cognition Layer: Cursor, ChatAid, Gamma → thinking & content agents
  • Memory Layer: Notebook LM → internal organizational memory
  • Automation Layer: Zapier, n8n, Gumloop → workflows that execute tasks
  • Insight Layer: Precision, Atlas → dashboards
  • Experience Layer: Chat interfaces, AI portals for employees

Live Walkthrough: Fathom records a sales call → Notebook LM ingests the summary → Zapier updates CRM → Precision displays real-time metrics → ChatAid generates content from internal memory.

Deliverables: AI Stack Diagram • Detailed workflow: "Event → Data → AI → Automation → Result"

Week 3

Intelligence in Action — Data → Decisions → Reflexes

Core Aim: Convert data into predictive dashboards & business reflexes.

Key Use Cases

  • Dynamic Pricing: Inputs (demand, seasonality, competition) → AI-recommended price ranges
  • Fraud & Risk Detection: Pattern detection, anomaly scoring
  • Performance Benchmarking: Weekly KPIs, predictive trends, early-risk alerts (churn prediction)

Decision Intelligence Blueprint Workshop: Students define which decisions need improvement, what data is needed, what metrics to track, what the dashboard looks like, and what action the AI takes.

Deliverables: Decision Intelligence Blueprint • First dashboard sketch • Updated AI Advantage Map

Week 4

The Age of Agents — Building Autonomous AI Teams

Core Aim: Build AI agents that can work like employees.

What is Agentic AI? Agents can perceive, think, plan, act, and learn.

  • Sales lead research agent
  • HR screening agent
  • Customer support triage bot
  • Marketing content generator
  • Finance forecasting agent

The 5-Part Agent Structure

  • Goal — What the agent is trying to achieve
  • Context / Knowledge — What it knows
  • Tools — What it can use
  • Actions — What it does
  • Boundaries & Escalation — When humans step in

Deliverables: Agent Design specification • Success metrics • Human-in-loop plan

Week 5

Governance & Experience — Building AI Systems Humans Trust

Core Aim: Deploy AI responsibly, with ethics, controls, and trust.

Governance Foundations

  • Bias & fairness
  • Transparency
  • Privacy & data security
  • Accountability
  • Kill-switches
  • Logging & monitoring

Creating Trusted AI Experiences

  • Clear boundaries ("I can help with X, not Y")
  • Confidence scores & explanation mechanisms
  • Error-handling gracefully
  • Human override options

Deliverables: Final AI Advantage Map • AI Stack Diagram • Decision Intelligence Blueprint • Agent & Governance Plan • Final capstone presentation

Outcomes

What You Leave With

Each participant leaves with a complete, actionable AI transformation plan:

  • AI Advantage Map — 5 actionable AI opportunities for your business
  • AI Tool Stack Diagram — Your organization's intelligence architecture
  • Decision Intelligence Blueprint — Data-to-decision pipelines
  • Agent Workflow + Governance Plan — Autonomous systems with human oversight
  • 90-Day AI Implementation Roadmap — For a real business
