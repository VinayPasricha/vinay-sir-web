Vinay Pasricha

Life and
Journey

From engineer to entrepreneur to builder. A chronicle of 35 years of identifying market gaps, building teams, and creating lasting impact across industries.

1987
Eicher Motors
The journey begins. An engineering graduate from PEC University steps into the corporate world at Eicher Motors. The seeds of understanding systems, manufacturing, and organizational design are planted here.
1990
Cube Communications
The entrepreneurial spark ignites. Cube Communications is founded, growing to 150 employees. Early lessons in leadership, team building, and the art of growing a business from nothing.
1998
Orange Technology
Leading a team of 80 programmers building enterprise software. Understanding the power of technology teams and the foundations that would later drive GoodSpace AI.
2003
WLC College India
A vision to transform education in India. WLC College India is established, focused on fashion, design, and business. What begins as an idea grows into an institution that will produce 50,000+ alumni.
2008
Scaling WLC
WLC expands dramatically. The team grows to 2,500 members. Thousands of students graduate each year, entering industries across India and beyond. Education becomes the legacy.
2012
Harvard Business School
The Owner/President Management program at Harvard Business School. Strategic frameworks, global perspectives, and connections with leaders from around the world refine the approach to building.
2015
50,000 Alumni
A milestone that matters. WLC College India reaches 50,000+ graduates. Fashion designers, business leaders, and creative professionals — all carrying forward the mission of quality education.
2020
GoodSpace AI
The convergence of technology and hiring. GoodSpace AI is born — an AI-powered platform that will go on to place 500,000+ candidates. Multilingual AI interviewers, automated recruitment at scale.
2024
AI For Business Leaders
The book is published. A practical guide drawing on 35 years of building, distilling frameworks for how organizations can think about and implement AI. Teaching begins alongside building.
2025
BOSS Platform
The frontier. An autonomous enterprise operating system. AI agents that handle outreach, support, and research. The single platform to iterate, evaluate, deploy, and monitor AI agents. The journey continues.
Today
The Forest
Grows
Builder, thinker, and creator. 35+ years of identifying market gaps and building solutions that create lasting impact. The paths continue to branch, the forest continues to grow, and new trails are always being explored.
